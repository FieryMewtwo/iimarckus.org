Battle Tower In‐Jokes

In Pokémon Crystal, some trainers in the Battle Tower are in‐jokes which refer to the developers. For instance:

  • “Guitarist Masuda,” one of the trainers, may be a reference to Jun’ichi Masuda, Pokemon’s music director.
  • “Bug Catcher Tajiri” is a reference to Pokemon’s creator, Satoshi Tajiri, and his childhood fixation with bugs.
  • “Scientist Mori” is named after Akito Mori, a programmer who works on the Pokémon games (specifically, in the Pokémon Diamond and Pearl credits he is listed under “Communication System Programming”).
  • “Teacher Kawakami” may be referring to Naoko Kawakami, who appears in the game credits. I do not know what role she played in development, only that she was listed under “Special Thanks.”
